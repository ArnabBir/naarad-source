Awaaz IIT Kharagpur

The students of IIT Kharagpur have taken an initiative to celebrate Independence Day with the deprived children of village Soladhar, around 1.5 km from Prem Bazaar gate. Students collected fund themselves and visited villages to celebrate Independence Day with those children.
This independence day, Students of IIT Kharagpur organized a Prabhat Pheri and encouraged villagers to join them. They unfurled flag, sung patriotic songs, cheered them for their performance and gave speeches to motivate to learn and be health conscious. Sweets, Fruits, Biscuits, Notebooks and pens were also distributed afterwards.
This social work has been undertaken by Hemant Singh, Sushant Suman, Ajay Kumar Singh, Deepak Verma, Amit Kumar Das, Tapasya Singh, Vishal Singh, Ashwinikant Tiwari, Yashwant Singh, Jagannath and some other students.
Awaaz,IIT Kharagpur wishes such attempts be taken on a regular basis for the benefits of the society.
